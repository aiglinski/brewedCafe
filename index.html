Located on Historic Brady Street, the warm and homey "Brewed Cafe" invites you to relax with an excellent quality coffee roasted at Milwaukee's (Anodyne Coffee Roasting Co.) . Enjoy an array of delicious foods ranging from home made bakery to our award winning vegetarian chili, add in signature sandwiches and spreads. Always healthy and fresh, Think olive oil !!!
Brewed's comfortable setting offers a meeting place to freely hangout and enjoy art and music. So, support friends and local business'...take the time...and take a friend.
